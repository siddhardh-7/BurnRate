AttributeTypeDescription

Narration: "The OpenTelemetry GenAI semantic conventions track everything about your AI agents — every input token, every output token, cache hits, reasoning tokens. Everything except a single dollar. When your agent runs wild at 3am, your observability stack tells you how many tokens it burned. Not how much money you lost."
